Who Am I

I'm soon to be a senior in high school at Spokane Valley Tech and I like to program stuff. My favorite thing to make, although I am still learning how it works, is game/rendering engines with C++ and OpenGL. In April/May 2020 I decided to put my knowledge of programming to the test by making a 2D Game Development API for C++. After about a month of work, I finished it. Although the result is rather disappointing and hard to work with, I learned a lot about the design of engines. My next one will likely be much better and include my newly learned physically based rendering pipeline. If you want to look at that project and my others, you can see them at my github.
